FleetDocs

Fleet & Driver Management System

Designed and developed a compliance-first platform for transport operations where fleet and driver documents are uploaded, scanned, and monitored in one place. Using AWS Textract (OCR), FleetDocs extracts critical metadata such as expiry dates, registration details, insurance data, and driver credentials, then validates it for real-time tracking. The system includes role-aware monitoring dashboards and an intelligent reminder pipeline that proactively pushes alerts before deadlines to avoid compliance gaps and legal risk.
